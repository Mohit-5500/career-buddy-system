Insurance Brokers

These are licensed professionals who offer insurance broking services to their clients. They are licensed to offer policies from any insurance company based on your requirements. There are three main types of insurance brokers: Direct insurance brokers reinsurance brokers and composite brokers.

Certified Financial Planner (CFP)

CFP is a certified expert who works in the area related to financial planning, insurance, taxes, estate planning and retirement. They help individuals with financial management. The most important aspect of these professionals is to be fiduciary of your assets.

Cost Accountant

A cost accountant is a professional who analyses product costs that are manufactured and sold by the organization. They inspect the cost in the production network and perform productivity examinations.
